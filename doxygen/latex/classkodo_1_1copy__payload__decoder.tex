\subsection{Detailed Description}
\subsubsection*{template$<$class Super\-Coder$>$class kodo\-::copy\-\_\-payload\-\_\-decoder$<$ Super\-Coder $>$}

Make decoder work on a copy of the payload. 

In the standard A\-P\-I the payload buffer may be modified during decoding, this can be problematic if you wish to e.\-g. pass the same payload to multiple decoders. To solve this you may use the \hyperlink{classkodo_1_1copy__payload__decoder}{copy\-\_\-payload\-\_\-decoder} layer to ensure that the payload is not modified by decoders.
